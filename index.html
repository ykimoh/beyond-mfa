Beyond

A tool that helps put inclusive and accessible design into action.

About

Beyond is an original research project and integrated prototype system for inclusive cross-platform UX design by Young (Yeon Kyung) Kim, completed in 2023 as the culminating project of the Master of Fine Arts in Interaction Design program at the School of Visual Arts in New York. The project addresses the field through an integrated set of practitioner tools: an automated accessibility audit, an education flow, an accessibility mode that simulates assistive-technology environments to verify focus order, landmarks, and alt text, and a testing platform that involves people with disabilities directly in the design process.

Beyond is grounded in original primary research with eight UX designers (four senior, four junior) and built against the WCAG 2.1 standard. It was completed under the guidance of Eric Forman (Major Professor, SVA Interaction Design faculty) and Elyse Voegeli (Thesis Advisor).
